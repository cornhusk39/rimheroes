# RimHeroes

D&D classes for RimWorld. Rare **Hero** colonists carry a Dungeons and Dragons class: they level from
1 to 20, cast real spells, wear class armor that grows with them, gather a retinue of little **mim**
followers, and delve dungeons for loot and legendary weapons.

Playtest alpha, in active development. Feedback and bug reports are welcome.

## Get it

The easy way is to subscribe on the Steam Workshop, which pulls in the dependencies for you.

**Requirements:** Harmony, the RimWorld DLCs you own, Vanilla Expanded Framework, and
[Mooloh's D&D Menagerie](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2751849453) (a hard
dependency). Load RimHeroes last.

## What's inside

- 12 playable D&D classes, each with its own features and a five-tier class armor set.
- More than 110 custom spells and abilities, 90 of them spells.
- Levels 1 to 20, with death saving throws so a downed hero rolls to hang on.
- 9 themed dungeons with traps, locked reliquaries, and bosses that scale to your colony.
- A retinue of mim companions that cook, haul, dig, heal, and fight.
- Druid wildshape into real creatures, up to a dragon.

## Coming soon

- **Subclasses.** Each class will branch into Dungeons and Dragons style subclasses with their own
  features. Not in yet, so every class currently plays as its base version.
